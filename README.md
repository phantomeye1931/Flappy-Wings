Ever thought the Elytra was too **overpowered**? Or just wanted an **incentive** to use **alternative transportation** methods, this might be the mod for you!

**Flappy Wings** replaces the behaviour of the Elytra from gliding to double jumping!

It features a completely fresh **Elytra Flap mechanic**, triggered by pressing space, replacing the gliding offered by vanilla. This feature is complete with **new sounds, particles**, and **tons of config options** to **customise** the mod's behaviour to your liking.


> ### ![Info symbol](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/9d817e28316429bbd45d2951e5d8d7fba618b4c9.png) Hot Tip
> This mod can also easily be used to **disable the Elytra altogether** by changing the server config. This can be configured **per dimension**, so it's possible to, for example, keep the gliding mechanic only in **The End**.


![Player playing flappy bird](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/8d5eb77759c7562de9d6ca82eda1eb94c645459c.png)


As well as **expanding on your current speed**, the Elytra flap boost also gives you a boost **in the direction you're looking**, for easy and responsive traversal.

The amount of vertical and horizontal boost also vary depending on your speed before boosting, creating a **dynamic system** where the **boost responds** to how you're moving through the air.


> ### ![Warning symbol](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/a7280c9afb6e5a98d68570ec7a3399bde8d4d76c.png) Warning
> 
> This mod can be **dangerously fun** to hop around with, and **might cause distraction** from projects. Use with caution.



<video src="https://phantomeye1931.github.io/video_uploads/flappy_wings_showcase.mp4" controls loop autoplay muted ></video>


Triggering an Elytra flap boost also ensures that the **fall damage** from hitting the ground afterwards takes the boost into account, such that you will **never take fall damage from any height gained by the boost**.

This makes sure it's relatively safe to use and your **toes stay intact**.



The total config options to customise this mod with include:

- Horizontal and vertical **boost amounts**
- Whether **fall damage** is mitigated
- What **dimensions** to be able to glide in
- Whether to enable **dynamic boosts**
- **Cooldown** amount between boosts



> ### ![Info symbol](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/9d817e28316429bbd45d2951e5d8d7fba618b4c9.png) Hot Tip
> By default, using the Elytra to glide is still possible in **the End**, but this is **fully configurable per dimension**, including **modded dimensions**!
